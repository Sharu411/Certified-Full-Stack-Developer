Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Coconut
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Instructions

  1. Mix the rice and coconut milk at the sim flavour to the 20 minutes
  2. After that grind the sugar on mixer
  3. Apply ghee on the rice and milk mixing
  4. At finally add the sugar and dip into the coconut grinder ,then eat
